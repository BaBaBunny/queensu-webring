What is a Webring?

The QueensU Webring connects student-built sites into one shared network. It helps visitors discover projects, portfolios, and stories from across the Queens community.

Each ring links out to individual sites while making it easy to hop between them. This keeps student work visible and encourages collaboration.

How to Join

Pick the webring that fits your program, then submit your site to the ring maintainer. Once approved, your site will appear in the directory and start receiving traffic from the ring.

Have a question or want to add a new ring? Reach out to the organizers and we will help you get set up.
